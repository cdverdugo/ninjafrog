Meet our character Ninja Frog!

After several adventures through many lands, Ninja Frog now finds himself in the desert in search of a mystical pyramid. According to legend, once all fruit has been collected, the pyramid will appear!


Ninja Frog searching the desert

As a level designer, I wanted to focus on a unique theme and style. I decided on a desert theme and found assests that aligned with the theme of the desert. Skeletons are the main obstacle that players need to navigate against and shoot their daggers to clear their path. Other assests consist of trees and bushes as well as a platform of sand and platforms of skulls that move vertically. After all 5 fruits are shot by the NinjaFrog's dagger, a pyramid will appear to move to the next level.
